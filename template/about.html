About Us

Cooking Since 2023

Welcome to SudhaStar, a leading brand committed to delivering high-quality, flavorful products. We proudly offer a diverse range of offerings through our two sub-brands:

Shwet: Offering freshly made batters for soft idlis and crispy dosas, crafted from the finest materials for a perfect, traditional experience. Our batters are designed to deliver consistent quality and authentic flavors with every use.

At SudhaStar, we also offer a selection of savory snacks, all prepared with the finest ingredients to guarantee freshness and taste.
